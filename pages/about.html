About

I create custom leather objects using medieval techniques, expressed through a contemporary lens. I specialize in mirrors, and also design lighting and tables. The work embraces raw leather’s mystique—witchy, gothy, and timeless—yet feels at home in both rustic interiors and cutting‑edge spaces.
